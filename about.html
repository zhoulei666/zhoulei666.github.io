My Album

Here are some interesting photos I took in the last 3 years. Part of it is travel in the UK or elsewhere. Another part is about my experience as an intern in China.

NOTE

P1 : I have been to London many times in the past 3 years and I took many pictures of the sights of this wonderful city.Included the British Museum, Towder Bridge and Westminster.I also visited the Holmes Museum and had fantastic memories of here. By the way, I took a long walk by the Thames and went to the London Eye as well.I must say it's quite a prefect trip to London.

P2 : The most artistic in London is free graffiti under the subway bridge. I like this artistic expression in public. These are usually not common in some countries such as China. These street cultures intoxicated me.

P3 : There are also some pictures of me in other interesting places such as Saipan. It is obvious that I am a person who likes to try new challenges. I have experiences such as driving an ATV, traveling by a helicopter or even diving. I also recorded them as photos.

P4 : I use my free vacation time. I once had some internship experience in a local TV station in my hometown (China). I learned how to be a media worker here. Mastered the use of professional software such as EDIUS to make TV shows. It also increased my social experience.
